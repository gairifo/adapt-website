Inside the AngularJS factory

AI converts your directives and services. Component by component. No big bang.

01

AngularJS parsing

Our AI ingests your AngularJS application — directives, controllers, services, factories, filters, templates, and routing configuration. It builds a complete component dependency graph. Every artifact is logged and inspectable.

02

Component generation

The AI emits idiomatic components in your chosen target — React (with hooks), Vue 3 (Composition API), or Angular 17+ (standalone components). AngularJS-specific patterns ($scope, $rootScope, $http, $q) are replaced with modern equivalents. Senior architects review every structural decision.

03

Incremental cutover

Components are migrated and deployed incrementally — new and legacy components coexist in the same application during transition. The AI generates behavioral tests comparing old and new components. Zero downtime. Zero big-bang risk.
