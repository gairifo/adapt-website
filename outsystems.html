Inside the OutSystems factory

AI converts your OML deterministically. Architects own the structure.

01

OML parsing

Our AI ingests your OutSystems OML files and builds a complete map of every entity, screen, business rule, integration, and data flow. Every artifact is logged and inspectable — nothing goes into the conversion as a black box.

02

High-code generation

The AI emits idiomatic, maintainable high-code in your chosen target stack — C#/.NET, Java/Spring, React, Node.js, or another modern framework. Senior architects review every structural decision. AI handles the volume; humans handle the judgment.

03

Equivalence testing

Before any cutover, the AI synthesizes behavioral tests that compare new system output against the live OutSystems system output. You migrate on a passing diff, not on faith.
